What is Astrology?

Astrology is a predictive science that studies the influence of cosmic objects like stars and planets on human lives. In astrology, there are 9 planets called Navagrahas, including the Sun, Moon, and others like Rahu and Ketu. Each planet affects life differently, and their positions in the 12 houses of a horoscope can bring positive or negative influences.

  • Sun (Surya), Moon (Chandra), Mars (Mangala)
  • Mercury (Budha), Jupiter (Brihaspati), Venus (Shukra)
  • Saturn (Shani), Rahu, and Ketu

The arrangement of these planets in one's Kundli (horoscope) dictates aspects of personality, life events, and challenges, influencing decisions and behaviors.
